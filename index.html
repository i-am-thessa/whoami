Who I am

Thessa is a Developer/Solutions Architect/Development Head specializing in Project Delivery with vast knowledge on different mobile, enterprise and cloud technologies. Her skillset ranges from development to testing to management. Anything needed to get things done and achieve excellence.

She is also a musician, an artist, an active sports enthusiast and a mother of 3 (almost 4) beautiful children.
